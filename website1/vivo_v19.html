Vivo V19 full specifications, Price: A quick review

The Chinese giant has launched the much anticipated Vivo V19 amid COVID-19 outbreak, delayed by about two weeks. The vivo V19 is a midrange high rated Smartphone with Qualcomm Snapdragon Octa-core processor and 8GB RAM operating on Android 10 with FunTouch OS 10. It comes in two storage variants 128GB and 256GB. However the Vivo V19 global variant launched on Monday is different in specifications from the Indonesian variant which was launched in last month. Let’s have a look at Vivo V19 full specifications and price. Vivo V19 full specifications

Processor: The Vivo V19 is powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 712, octa-core (2x2.3 GHz Kryo 360 Gold and 6x1.7 GHz Kryo 360 Silver) processor and Adreno 616 GPU.

RAM: 8GB

OS: Android 10.0 with Funtouch 10.0

Storage: 128 GB/ 256 GB, with dedicated slot for Micro SD card

Display: The Vivo V19 boasts with 6.44 inches full HD (1080x2400 pixels) super AMOLED capacitive touchscreen with 409 ppi pixel density.

Camera:

Rear Camera: Quad camera (48MP + 8MP + 2MP + 2MP) with LED Flash

· 48MP primary camera with f/1.79 aperture

· 8MP wide angle camera with f/2.2 aperture

· 2MP camera f/2.4 aperture

· 2MP depth camera with f/2.4 aperture

Front Camera: 32MP + 8MP dual camera

· 32MP primary camera with f/2.0 aperture

· 8MP wide angle (105° field of view) camera with f/2.2 aperture

Network: Dual SIM (nano), 4G, 3G, 2G

4G Bands: TD-LTE 2600(band 38) / 2300(band 40) / 2500(band 41) / 1900(band 39) FD-LTE 2100(band 1) / 1800(band 3) / 2600(band 7) / 900(band 8) / 700(band 28) / 850(band 5) / 800(band 20)

3G Bands: UMTS 1900 / 2100 / 850 / 900 MHz

2G Bands: GSM 1800 / 1900 / 850 / 900 MHz

Connectivity: WiFi 802.11 a/b/g/n, Bluetooth 5.0, GPS with A-GPS, USB OTG, Type C connector

Battery: 4500mAh non-removable Li-Po battery with Flash charging 33 W (Vivo Flash Charge 2.0), which can recharge the phone upto 54% in 30 minutes.

Sensors: Fingerprint, Accelerometer, Gyro, Proximity and Compass Sensor.

Dimensions & Weight: 159.64 x 75.04 x 8.5 mm, 186.5 grams

Colors: Sleek Silver, Gleam Black

Vivo V19 Price: Approx 25,990 INR

The Vivo V19 has been launched and expected to be available by 6th May 2020. The Vivo V19 is expected to be priced in mid range and will be a good buy once available.
