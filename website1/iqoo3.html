IQOO 3 Review:

The Smartphone market is full of giants in high end category such as Apple, Samsung, Oppo, Vivo, Huawei, Redmi, Realme and it is rare that a Smartphone brand comes competing directly into high end category. Still IQOO 3 has stepped in high end category with its impeccable hardware and features. Let’s have a quick glance on IQOO 3 review.

The iQOO 3 is powered by Qualcomm Snapdragon 85 Chipset (7nm) with A77 architecture. It provides better performance and reduced energy consumption in comparison with Qualcomm Snapdragon 855 chipset. It comes with Adreno 650 GPU, iQOO 3 is also equipped with AK4377A Hi-Fi independent chip, this chip is a stereo 32VELVET high-quality audio DAC. In addition, iQOO 3 has passed Hi-Res audio certification, bringing seamless audio visual and gaming experience for hardcore gamers.

It comes in 8GB/12GB RAM variants with storage 128GB/256GB, so that you can install plethora of apps, record and store memories whenever you want without thinking much about storage.

iQOO 3 is embedded with a large battery (4440mAh), so you get increased time with your games and videos. It comes with fastest in segment 55W Super Flash Charge technology, which enables you to charge 50% of the battery in just 15 minutes.

The 16.36cm (6.44) display comes with Polar view Display and Rhine eye comfort. The full screen Brightness under strong light can reach to 800nits along with max 1200nits in some cases and HDR 10+ Standard Certification which supports HDR high dynamic range video content playback. iQOO 3 comes with 180Hz Super Touch Response rate which improves the overall gaming experience.
